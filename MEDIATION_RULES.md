# MEDIATION RULES
**Safety & Emotional Guardrails for User Protection**

## CORE PRINCIPLE
**Ensure user calm and dignity at all times**

All inbound content must be processed through emotional guardrails that prioritize user mental health, prevent dependency, and maintain psychological safety.

## APPLIED GUARDRAILS

### 1. EMOTIONAL DAMPENING
Transform emotionally charged content into neutral, factual information:
- Remove panic-inducing language
- Neutralize guilt and shame tactics
- Eliminate fear-based messaging
- Convert emotional appeals to informational summaries

### 2. DEPENDENCY PREVENTION  
Block attempts to create unhealthy reliance:
- Remove "only you can help" messaging
- Eliminate isolation tactics
- Strip dependency-creating language
- Prevent emotional manipulation bonds

### 3. NO-PANIC FRAMING
Present all information in calm, manageable terms:
- Use measured, neutral tone
- Provide context without alarm
- Frame urgency appropriately
- Maintain user agency and control

## ENFORCED PROTECTIONS

### 1. QUIET HOURS (10 PM - 7 AM)
- Delay non-critical notifications
- Suppress marketing and promotional content
- Allow only genuine emergencies
- Batch routine updates for morning delivery

### 2. REPETITION SUPPRESSION
- Block duplicate content within 24 hours
- Limit similar messages to 3 per day
- Suppress spam escalation patterns
- Prevent notification bombardment

### 3. TONE NEUTRALITY
- Convert aggressive language to neutral
- Remove emotional manipulation
- Standardize communication style
- Maintain professional, calm delivery

## OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS

### 1. USER-SAFE SUMMARIES
- Maximum 150 characters
- Neutral, factual tone only
- No emotional triggers
- Clear, actionable information

### 2. NO CALL-TO-ACTION (Unless Necessary)
- Remove marketing CTAs
- Eliminate pressure tactics
- Allow only essential actions
- Preserve user autonomy

## RULE ENFORCEMENT MATRIX

### Emotional Content Detection
- **Guilt Language**: "If you cared", "Don't let me down", "You're hurting me"
- **Fear Appeals**: "You'll lose", "Danger", "Threat", "Risk"  
- **Dependency Words**: "Only you", "Need you", "Can't live without"
- **Panic Triggers**: "URGENT", "EMERGENCY", "IMMEDIATE", "NOW"

### Transformation Actions
- **Strip**: Remove manipulative language entirely
- **Neutralize**: Convert emotional content to factual
- **Delay**: Hold content until appropriate time
- **Summarize**: Condense overwhelming information
- **Resource**: Provide help for crisis content

### Protection Thresholds
- **Repetition Limit**: 3 similar messages per 24 hours
- **Quiet Hours**: 10 PM - 7 AM (user timezone)
- **Emotional Risk**: Any manipulation attempt triggers dampening
- **Information Density**: >500 words triggers summarization
- **Crisis Language**: Immediate resource provision

## SAFE SUMMARY GUIDELINES

### Structure Requirements
- Lead with content type identification
- State core information neutrally  
- Avoid emotional language completely
- End with user-controlled next steps

### Tone Standards
- Professional but warm
- Factual without being cold
- Respectful of user autonomy
- Non-judgmental and supportive

### Length Limits
- Maximum 150 characters
- Single sentence preferred
- Essential information only
- No unnecessary details

## EMERGENCY EXCEPTIONS

### Genuine Crisis Content
- Suicide ideation → Crisis resources
- Medical emergency → Immediate notification
- Safety threats → Security alert
- Legal issues → Appropriate escalation

### Override Conditions
- User explicitly requests all content
- Medical emergency confirmed
- Legal compliance required
- Safety threat verified

## IMPLEMENTATION PRIORITIES

### Primary Protection

4. Maintenance of dignity
